## Weekend Discovery
- As a Weekend Planner, I want to see events in Manhattan on Saturday afternoon so I can plan my day.
- As a Family Organizer, I want only children-welcome events so I can bring my kids.
- As a First-Time Visitor, I want a short list of must-see sites.

## Scheduling and Routing
- As a Weekend Planner, I want a suggested order of events with travel guidance.
- As a Family Organizer, I want to avoid long travel legs and large gaps.

## Trivia and History
- As an Architecture Enthusiast, I want short historical context for each event.
- As a First-Time Visitor, I want to learn why a site is notable.

## Yearlong Navigation
- As an Architecture Enthusiast, I want to find events outside the OHNY weekend.
- As a First-Time Visitor, I want upcoming events over the next month.

## Site and Resources
- As a user, I want to understand ticketing, lottery, and reservation rules.
- As a user, I want basic OHNY contact and FAQ information.

## Future Admin Expansion
- As an OHNY staff member, I want to correct event data and update tags.
- As an OHNY staff member, I want to publish special notices or policy updates.

## Open questions
- Which user stories are critical for launch vs. nice-to-have for phase 1?
- Should admin stories remain informational or include workflow expectations?
